About Us

MAKS CUB3E Covid 19 Voice BOT is a completely NLP based convertational AI. It is the world's first voice BOT which is capable of giving information in Bengali, and with Bengali it is also capable of giving answer two more different languages - English and Hindi. Main moto of this voicebot is to prevenet spreading fake newses and giving proper information about COVID19 to common people. The BOT is trained on the guidelines and given data of WHO.
